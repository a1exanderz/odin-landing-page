We have so many questions about our mental health: Do I have depression or should I just "think more positively"? Do I have ADHD or am I just lazy? Unfortunately, there exist few online resources that can easily answer such questions, and therapy can be hard to obtain. Garden Of Your Mind aims to provide free, personalized, and high-quality mental health resources to the world.
